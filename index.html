About Us | See - Play - Stay

In the last 12 months I have found myself travelling much more than I have done previously. One of the places I keep returning to is Berlin! Berlin is the vibrant capital of Germany, a city rich in history and culture.


See - Play - Stay is a travel website created with gamers in mind! While you travel, check out some of Berlin's most amazing landmarks by heading over to our See Page

Need a break from all the sight-seeing? To check out some amazing arcades and gaming cafes, head on over to our Play page

Of course, you're going to need a place to stay, whether you are exhausted from the sight-seeing and shopping, or you just need to relax after a rough gaming session. Head on over to our Stay page


Berlin is filled with iconic landmarks and there are endless sights to see and things to do. Whether you want to go on a shopping spree at Alexanderplatz or Potsdamer Platz, or do some sight-seeing. From Brandenburg gate to Museum Island, you're sure to find something to suit your fancy.
