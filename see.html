Things to see while you are in Berlin!

As you know, being a gamer is a full time affair. While you've taken the time to visit a new city, why not also take the time to touch some grass. You know, like you're told to do when you're winning a game of League of Legends or Valorant. Prove the haters wrong, you HAVE touched grass recently!

Brandenburg Gate

While you're in Berlin, why not visit Brandenburg Gate, an 18th-century neoclassical monument in Berlin, built on the orders of the King of Prussia Frederick William II after restoring the Orangist power by suppressing the Dutch popular unrest.

Berlin TV Tower

While you're in Berlin, check out the Berlin TV Tower. This is a 368m-tall tower, opened in 1969, with a viewing gallery (Tower tour Experience available) at 203m, and the opportunity to dine in the revolving restaurant at 207m.

Museum Island

Why not also visit one of the most visited sights, the Museum Island of Berlin. This is a museum complex located on the northern part of Spree Island, right in the heart of Berlin

Berlin Cathedral

While you're in Berlin and visiting Museum Island, you can also check out the Berlin Cathedral. Constructed in 1905 and also known as the Evangelical Supreme Parish and Collegiate Church, located on Museum Island.
